OUR OBJECTIVES

The Objective of the institution is to provide good infrastructural facilities like very well qualified faculty, well equipped laboratories, library, and computing facilities to train the engineering students and build a sound technological background to help them to meet the requirements of the advanced industries and research organizations at national and international levels

VISION & Mission

The Vision of the Institute is to identify the hidden engineering talent and to develop human resource capital in the technical field of education, and to facilitate the core competency in accordance with the company and industrial standards by meeting the persistent demands of the industries in the recent policies of globalization, which require specialized expertise and sophisticated instrumentation in the area of technology. "To build a sound research based technological background"
The Mission of the Institute is to build a sound research based technological background amongst the students to precisely bridge the gap between the industrial organizations and the academic institutions leading to emerge with right selection of technology, which an end user viably can best afford economically. "To bridge the gap between the industrial organizations and the academic institutions"
